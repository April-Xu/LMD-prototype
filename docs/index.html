We Make Reflection Easy in Three Steps

EMOJI
Students choose an emoji that represents how they feel about the latest milestone.

PROGRESS BAR
Students estimate how well they achieved the learning goals with progress bars.

REFLECTION PROMPTS
Students then captures a short reflection on the milestone. They can write, sketch, or record audio.
